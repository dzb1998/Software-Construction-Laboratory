gitk description 

TOP:
1. The upper rightmost window show the date of the commit in the format 'YYYY-MM-DD'
and the time in the military time format 'HH:MM'.

2. The upper middle window show the authors and their email address of each commit.

3. The upper left window shows all of the commits made in a hierarchical tree
and also showing their relationships, and the HEAD pointer, and commit messages.

Middle:
4. The section between the upper half and the lower half has the SHA1 ID, 
and a search box with various options.

Bottom:
5. The lower right window shows the files impacted by the selected commit.

6. The lower left window shows a lot of information of the selected commit, such as
the author, committer, branches, commit message, the changes in diff format, etc.
